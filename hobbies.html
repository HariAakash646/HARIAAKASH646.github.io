My Hobbies & Interests

Cricket

Cricket isn't just a sport for me; it's a passion that has been with me since childhood. Whether I'm playing with friends on weekends or watching intense international matches, cricket always manages to bring excitement and joy to my life. The strategic aspects of the game, combined with the skill and athleticism required, make it endlessly fascinating to me.

Why I Love Cricket:

  • The perfect blend of individual performance and team strategy
  • The electric atmosphere during India-Pakistan matches
  • Playing as a middle-order batsman in college tournaments
  • Following the IPL every season and supporting Chennai Super Kings

Basketball

Basketball entered my life during high school and quickly became one of my favorite activities. The fast-paced nature of the game, the teamwork required, and the constant action make it incredibly engaging. I play point guard in casual games with friends, and I've developed a decent three-point shot over the years of practice.

My Basketball Journey:

  • Playing in weekend leagues with friends and colleagues
  • Following the NBA and supporting the Golden State Warriors
  • Learning new techniques and improving my shooting form
  • The thrill of close games and last-minute shots

Sci-Fi & Fantasy

I've been a dedicated sci-fi and fantasy enthusiast since I discovered Asimov's Foundation series as a teenager. These genres have shaped my imagination, offered escape, and provided interesting thought experiments about technology, society, and human potential. Whether it's exploring distant galaxies or magical realms, I'm always ready for the next adventure.

Favorite Worlds to Explore:

  • The intricate world-building of Dune and The Wheel of Time
  • The philosophical questions raised in works like The Left Hand of Darkness
  • Epic fantasy series that blend magic and politics like A Song of Ice and Fire
  • Shows that reimagine humanity's future like Star Trek and The Expanse
